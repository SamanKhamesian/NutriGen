Creating a meal plan that fits the requirements and utilizes the available items can be a fun challenge. Here are three different meal plan options:

### Meal Plan Option 1

#### Breakfast: Eggs Benedict with Avocado
- **Calories:** 450 kcal
- **Protein:** 20g
- **Sugar:** 2g

**Portion Size:**
- 1 serving of Eggs Benedict with 1/2 avocado

**Recipe:**
1. Poach two eggs.
2. Toast an English muffin.
3. Top each half with a slice of Canadian bacon, a poached egg, and a drizzle of hollandaise sauce.
4. Slice half an avocado and serve on the side.

#### Lunch: Angus Beef Burger with Coleslaw
- **Calories:** 700 kcal
- **Protein:** 35g
- **Sugar:** 8g

**Portion Size:**
- 1 beef Angus burger patty (150g), 1 hamburger bun, 1 cup coleslaw

**Recipe:**
1. Grill the beef patty for about 5 minutes on each side.
2. Assemble the burger with the patty, lettuce, tomato, and a hamburger bun.
3. Serve with coleslaw on the side.

#### Dinner: Tofu Pad Thai
- **Calories:** 650 kcal
- **Protein:** 18g
- **Sugar:** 10g

**Portion Size:**
- 1 serving of Tofu Pad Thai

**Recipe:**
1. Stir-fry tofu with vegetables such as bell peppers and carrots.
2. Add cooked rice noodles and Pad Thai sauce.
3. Top with peanuts and lime juice.

#### Snacks: Maple Crème Brûlée & Starbucks Strawberry Acai Refresher
- **Calories:** 337.41 kcal
- **Protein:** 1.62g
- **Sugar:** 21.73g

**Portion Size:**
- 1 serving of Maple Crème Brûlée, 1 Starbucks Strawberry Acai Refresher

**Recipe:**
1. Prepare Maple Crème Brûlée by combining cream, sugar, and maple syrup, baking until set, and caramelizing the top with sugar.
2. Enjoy with a chilled Starbucks Strawberry Acai Refresher.

**Total for the Day:**
- **Calories:** 2137.41 kcal
- **Protein:** 74.62g
- **Sugar:** 41.73g

- **Protein:** 18g
- **Sugar:** 4g

**Portion Size:**
