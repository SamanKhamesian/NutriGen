**Meal Plan Option 1:**
- Breakfast: Eggs Benedict (1 serving) - 650 kcal
- Lunch: Avocado Toast (2 slices) - 500 kcal
- Dinner: Beef Angus Burger Patty with Baked Cheddar Ruffles (1 serving) - 800 kcal
- Snack: Chocolate Milkshake (1 serving) - 304 kcal

**Total for Meal Plan Option 1:**
- Total Calories: 2254 kcal
- Total Protein: 67g
- Total Sugar: 46g

**Recipe:**
- **Eggs Benedict:** Prepare poached eggs and place them on top of toasted English muffins with ham and hollandaise sauce.
- **Avocado Toast:** Mash avocado on whole-grain toast and top with desired seasonings like salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes.
- **Beef Angus Burger Patty:** Grill the beef patty to your preferred doneness and serve with baked cheddar ruffles.
- **Chocolate Milkshake:** Blend chocolate ice cream, milk, and chocolate syrup until smooth.
