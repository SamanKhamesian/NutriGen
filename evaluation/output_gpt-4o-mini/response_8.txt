### Meal Plan Option 1

- **Calories:** 400 kcal
- **Protein:** 5g
- **Sugar:** 1g
- **Recipe:** Mash the avocado and season with salt and lime. Serve with tortilla chips.

#### Dinner: Beef Angus Burger
- **Ingredients:**
  - 1 Angus beef burger patty (150g)
  - 1 hamburger bun
  - Lettuce and tomato slices
- **Calories:** 704 kcal
- **Protein:** 25g
- **Sugar:** 3g
- **Recipe:** Grill the beef patty for about 5 minutes on each side. Assemble the burger with the bun, lettuce, and tomato.
