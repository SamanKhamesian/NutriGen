### Meal Plan Option 1

**Breakfast: Eggs Benedict**  
- **Portion:** 1 serving  
- **Calories:** 400 kcal

- **Sugar:** 2g

**Lunch: Beef Angus Burger**  
- **Portion:** 1 burger with a 100g beef patty and bun  
- **Calories:** 500 kcal  
- **Protein:** 25g  
- **Sugar:** 6g

**Dinner: Tater Tots with Cheddar Chex Mix**  
- **Portion:** 2 servings of tater tots (approximately 200g), 1 serving of Cheddar Chex Mix  
- **Calories:** 450 kcal  
- **Protein:** 5g  
- **Sugar:** 10g  
- **Recipe:** Bake tater tots according to package instructions until golden and crispy. Serve with Cheddar Chex Mix on the side.  

**Snacks: Glazed Donut and Cranberry Juice**

- **Sugar:** 18g
