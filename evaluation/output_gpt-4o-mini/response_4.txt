### Meal Plan Option 1

#### Breakfast: Eggs Benedict
- **Ingredients**: 
  - 1 serving Eggs Benedict (1 portion)
  - 1 slice White Bread Toast (0.5 servings)
- **Total Calories**: 450 kcal
- **Protein**: 20g
- **Sugar**: 2g
- **Preparation**: 
  - Toast the bread. Prepare the poached eggs and hollandaise sauce per package instructions. Assemble by placing the toasted bread, topped with ham, poached eggs, and hollandaise sauce.

#### Lunch: Tofu Pad Thai
- **Ingredients**: 
  - 1 serving Tofu Pad Thai (0.5 servings)
  - 1/2 Avocado (1.25 servings)
- **Total Calories**: 600 kcal
- **Protein**: 15g
- **Sugar**: 5g
- **Preparation**: 
  - Prepare the Tofu Pad Thai according to package instructions. Slice the avocado and serve on the side.

#### Snack: Guacamole and Tortilla Chips
- **Ingredients**: 
  - 1 Guacamole Bowl (0.5 servings)
  - 1 serving Tortilla Chips (0.5 servings)
- **Total Calories**: 350 kcal
- **Protein**: 5g
- **Sugar**: 2g
- **Preparation**: 
  - Serve the guacamole in a bowl and enjoy with tortilla chips.

#### Dinner: Angus Beef Burger with Guacamole
- **Ingredients**: 
  - 1 Beef Angus Burger Patty (1 serving)
  - 1/2 Guacamole (0.5 servings)
  - 1 slice Tomato (0.5 servings)
  - 1/2 serving Fajita Veggies (0.5 servings)
- **Total Calories**: 650 kcal
- **Protein**: 25g
- **Sugar**: 3g
- **Preparation**: 
  - Grill the beef patty for 5 minutes. Assemble the burger with guacamole, tomato, and fajita veggies.

### Daily Totals for Option 1:
- **Total Calories**: 2050 kcal
- **Total Protein**: 65g
- **Total Sugar**: 12g
