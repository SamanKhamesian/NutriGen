Here are three different meal plan options that meet your targets for total daily calories, protein, and sugar while providing a balanced nutritional profile.

### Meal Plan Option 1

#### Breakfast: Everything Bagel with Cream Cheese
- **1 Everything Bagel (100g)** - 250 kcal
- **2 tablespoons Cream Cheese (30g)** - 100 kcal

**Preparation:** 
1. Toast the bagel until golden brown.
2. Spread the cream cheese evenly over each half.

**Total for Breakfast: 350 kcal**

#### Snack: Strawberry Acai Refresher
- **1 Starbucks Strawberry Acai Refresher (16 fl oz)** - 140 kcal

**Preparation:** 
1. Pour the refresher into a glass and enjoy chilled.

**Total for Snack: 140 kcal**

#### Lunch: Tofu Pad Thai
- **1 serving Tofu Pad Thai (200g)** - 400 kcal

**Preparation:**
1. Heat a pan with a little oil, add tofu, and sauté for 5 minutes.
2. Add the Pad Thai noodles and sauce, mixing well until heated through.

**Total for Lunch: 400 kcal**

#### Snack: Baked Beans
- **1/2 cup Baked Beans (120g)** - 150 kcal

**Preparation:**
1. Heat the baked beans in a saucepan over medium heat until warmed through.

**Total for Snack: 150 kcal**

#### Dinner: Angus Beef Burger with Coleslaw
- **1 Beef Angus Burger Patty (150g)** - 350 kcal
- **1 cup Coleslaw (100g)** - 200 kcal

**Preparation:**
1. Grill the beef patty for about 5 minutes on each side or until fully cooked.
2. Serve the patty in a bun (optional) and top with coleslaw.

**Total for Dinner: 550 kcal**

### Daily Totals:
- **Calories:** 1,590 kcal
- **Protein:** 51.45g
- **Sugar:** 54.41g
