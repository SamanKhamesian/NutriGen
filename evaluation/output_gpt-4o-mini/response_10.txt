### Meal Plan Option 1

**Breakfast: Eggs Benedict**  
- **Portion**: 1 serving (2 halves)  
- **Calories**: 450 kcal  
- **Protein**: 20g  
- **Sugar**: 2g  
**Recipe**:  
1. Poach 2 eggs until the whites are set.  
2. Toast 1 English muffin and place 1 slice of Canadian bacon on each half.  
3. Top each half with a poached egg.  
4. Drizzle with hollandaise sauce (store-bought or homemade).  

**Lunch: Tofu Pad Thai**  
- **Portion**: 1 serving (1 bowl)  
- **Calories**: 350 kcal  
- **Protein**: 16g  
- **Sugar**: 5g  
**Recipe**:  
1. Cook rice noodles according to package instructions.  
2. In a pan, sauté tofu until golden, then add cooked noodles, bean sprouts, and pad Thai sauce.  
3. Toss until heated through. Serve with lime wedges.  

**Dinner: Beef Angus Burger**  
- **Portion**: 1 burger (150g patty with bun and toppings)  
- **Calories**: 600 kcal  
- **Protein**: 35g  
- **Sugar**: 6g  
**Recipe**:  
1. Grill the beef patty for about 5 minutes on each side until cooked.  
2. Assemble the burger with a bun, lettuce, tomato, and any preferred condiments.  

**Snacks: Everything Bagel with Cream Cheese**  
- **Portion**: 1 bagel  
- **Calories**: 250 kcal  
- **Protein**: 10g  
- **Sugar**: 3g  
**Recipe**:  
1. Toast the bagel until golden brown.  
2. Spread cream cheese on both halves.  

**Total Nutritional Information**:  
- **Total Calories**: 1650 kcal  
- **Total Protein**: 81g  
- **Total Sugar**: 16g
