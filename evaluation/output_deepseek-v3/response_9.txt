### Meal Plan Option 1

#### Breakfast: Everything Bagel with Cream Cheese (400 kcal)
- **Portion:** 1 Everything Bagel (100g) with 2 tablespoons of cream cheese (30g).
- **Recipe:** Toast the bagel until golden brown. Spread cream cheese evenly on both halves.

#### Lunch: Tofu Pad Thai (450 kcal)
- **Portion:** 0.5 serving of Tofu Pad Thai (200g).
- **Recipe:** Heat the Pad Thai in a skillet over medium heat for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.

#### Snack: Starbucks Strawberry Acai Refresher (200 kcal)
- **Portion:** 2.0 servings (500ml).
- **Recipe:** Serve chilled over ice.

#### Dinner: Beef Angus Burger with Coleslaw (617.16 kcal)
- **Portion:** 1 Beef Angus Burger Patty (150g) with a bun, lettuce, and 2 servings of coleslaw (150g).
- **Recipe:** Grill the patty for 5 minutes on each side. Assemble the burger with lettuce and the bun. Serve with coleslaw on the side.

**Total Daily Nutrition:**
- Calories: 1667.16 kcal
- Protein: 51.45g
- Sugar: 54.41g

### Meal Plan Option 2

#### Breakfast: Baked Beans on Toast (350 kcal)
- **Portion:** 0.5 serving of Baked Beans (200g) on 1 slice of whole-grain toast (30g).
- **Recipe:** Heat the baked beans in a saucepan until warm. Toast the bread and top with the beans.

#### Lunch: Everything Bagel with Smoked Salmon (400 kcal)
- **Portion:** 1 Everything Bagel (100g) with 2 ounces of smoked salmon (50g) and a light spread of cream cheese (15g).
- **Recipe:** Toast the bagel. Spread a thin layer of cream cheese and layer the smoked salmon on top.

#### Snack: Corn Salsa with Tortilla Chips (200 kcal)
- **Portion:** 2 servings of Corn Salsa (100g) with 10 tortilla chips (30g).
- **Recipe:** Serve the salsa chilled with tortilla chips on the side.

#### Dinner: Maple Crème Brûlée with a Side Salad (717.16 kcal)

**Total Daily Nutrition:**
- Calories: 1667.16 kcal
- Protein: 51.45g
