### **Meal Plan Option 1**

#### **Breakfast: Eggs Benedict (340 kcal)**  
- **Portion**: 1 serving of Eggs Benedict (includes 1 poached egg, 1 English muffin, and 1 tbsp hollandaise sauce).  
- **Recipe**: Poach an egg for 3 minutes. Toast an English muffin. Assemble with a slice of ham or smoked salmon, the poached egg, and drizzle with hollandaise sauce.  

#### **Lunch: Beef Angus Burger with Coleslaw (520 kcal)**  
- **Portion**: 1 Beef Angus Burger (150g patty, bun, lettuce, and tomato) + 1 cup coleslaw.  
- **Recipe**: Grill the beef patty for 5 minutes. Assemble the burger with lettuce, tomato, and a bun. Serve with a side of coleslaw.  

#### **Snack: Avocado Toast (220 kcal)**  
- **Portion**: 1 slice of toasted bread topped with ½ avocado (mashed) and a sprinkle of salt and pepper.  
- **Recipe**: Toast a slice of bread. Mash ½ avocado and spread it on the toast. Season with salt and pepper.  

#### **Dinner: Tofu Pad Thai (1057 kcal)**  
- **Portion**: 1 serving of Tofu Pad Thai (includes rice noodles, tofu, peanuts, and vegetables).  
- **Recipe**: Cook rice noodles according to package instructions. Sauté tofu and vegetables in a pan. Add noodles and toss with Pad Thai sauce. Garnish with crushed peanuts.  

#### **Total for the Day**:  
- **Calories**: 2137 kcal  
- **Protein**: 52.62g  
- **Sugar**: 41.73g  
- **Fat**: 98g  
- **Carbohydrates**: 230g
