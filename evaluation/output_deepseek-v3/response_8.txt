### Meal Plan Option 1  
**Breakfast: Eggs Benedict (400 kcal)**  
- Recipe: Poach 2 eggs and place them on top of 1 toasted English muffin (60g) with 1 slice of Canadian bacon (30g). Drizzle with 2 tbsp of hollandaise sauce (30g).  

**Lunch: Garden Pizza Slice (450 kcal)**  
- Recipe: Heat 1 slice of Garden Pizza (150g) in the oven at 375°F for 10 minutes. Serve with a side of 1 cup of mixed greens (30g) dressed with 1 tsp olive oil and a squeeze of lemon.  

**Snack: Chocolate Milkshake (300 kcal)**  
- Recipe: Blend 1 cup of vanilla ice cream (130g) with 1/2 cup of whole milk (120ml) and 1 tbsp of chocolate syrup (20g).  

**Dinner: Angus Beef Burger with Avocado (700 kcal)**  
- Recipe: Grill a 150g Angus beef patty and assemble with 1 whole wheat bun (50g), 1 slice of cheddar cheese (30g), 1/2 sliced avocado (50g), lettuce, and tomato. Serve with 1 cup of baked cheddar Ruffles (30g).  

**Snack: Milano Double Chocolate Cookie (100 kcal)**  
- Recipe: Enjoy 0.5 of a Milano Double Chocolate Cookie (15g).  

**Totals:**  
- Calories: 2250 kcal  
- Protein: 67g  
- Sugar: 47g

### Meal Plan Option 2  
**Breakfast: Avocado Toast with Eggs (450 kcal)**  
- Recipe: Mash 1/2 avocado (50g) and spread it on 2 slices of whole-grain toast (60g). Top with 2 scrambled eggs (100g) and a sprinkle of salt and pepper.  

**Lunch: Angus Beef Burger with Tortilla Chips (600 kcal)**  
- Recipe: Grill a 150g Angus beef patty and assemble with 1 whole wheat bun (50g), lettuce, tomato, and 1 slice of cheddar cheese (30g). Serve with 1 cup of tortilla chips (30g).  

**Snack: Chocolate Milkshake (300 kcal)**  
- Recipe: Blend 1 cup of vanilla ice cream (130g) with 1/2 cup of whole milk (120ml) and 1 tbsp of chocolate syrup (20g).  

**Dinner: Garden Pizza Slice with Salad (600 kcal)**  
- Recipe: Heat 1 slice of Garden Pizza (150g) in the oven at 375°F for 10 minutes. Serve with a side of 1 cup of mixed greens (30g) dressed with 1 tsp olive oil and a squeeze of lemon.  

**Snack: Milano Double Chocolate Cookie (100 kcal)**  
- Recipe: Enjoy 0.5 of a Milano Double Chocolate Cookie (15g).  

**Totals:**  
- Calories: 2250 kcal  
- Protein: 66g  
- Sugar: 46g
